# About ClickChutney Analytics

## Our Mission

ClickChutney Analytics was born from a simple belief: **website analytics shouldn't come at the cost of user privacy**. We're building the future of web analytics - one that respects user privacy while providing the insights businesses need to grow.

## Why ClickChutney?

In a world where user privacy is increasingly important, traditional analytics platforms collect vast amounts of personal data. We believe there's a better way.

### Privacy-First Approach
- **No Personal Data Collection**: We don't track individuals across the web
- **Anonymous Analytics**: All data is aggregated and anonymized
- **GDPR Compliant**: Built with privacy regulations in mind
- **Transparent Practices**: Clear about what we collect and why

### Developer-Friendly
- **Easy Integration**: Single line of code to get started
- **Multiple Platforms**: React, Next.js, vanilla HTML, WordPress
- **Real-time Data**: See visitor activity as it happens
- **Powerful API**: Full programmatic access to your data

## Our Story

Founded in 2024, ClickChutney Analytics emerged from the growing need for privacy-respecting analytics tools. Our founding team, with backgrounds in software engineering and data privacy, saw firsthand how existing solutions failed to balance useful insights with user privacy.

### The Name
"ClickChutney" represents our philosophy: just like how chutney enhances a meal without overwhelming it, our analytics enhance your understanding of your audience without invading their privacy.
