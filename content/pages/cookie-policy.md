# Cookie Policy

**Effective Date:** January 1, 2025  
**Last Updated:** January 15, 2025

## What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are stored on your device when you visit a website. They help websites remember information about your visit, such as your preferences and login status, making your next visit easier and more useful.

## How We Use Cookies

ClickChutney Analytics uses cookies and similar technologies to:

- **Authenticate Users**: Keep you logged in to your dashboard
- **Remember Preferences**: Store your theme, language, and display settings
- **Analyze Usage**: Understand how our platform is used to improve the experience
- **Ensure Security**: Protect against fraud and unauthorized access
- **Provide Functionality**: Enable core platform features and functionality

## Types of Cookies We Use

### Essential Cookies
These cookies are necessary for the platform to function properly and cannot be disabled.

| Cookie Name | Purpose | Duration | Type |
|-------------|---------|----------|------|
| `auth-token` | User authentication | Session | Essential |
| `csrf-token` | Security protection | Session | Essential |
| `session-id` | Session management | 7 days | Essential |

### Functional Cookies
These cookies enable enhanced functionality and personalization.

| Cookie Name | Purpose | Duration | Type |
|-------------|---------|----------|------|
| `theme-preference` | Remember theme choice | 1 year | Functional |
| `sidebar-state` | Remember sidebar preferences | 30 days | Functional |
| `dashboard-layout` | Remember dashboard layout | 90 days | Functional |

### Analytics Cookies
These cookies help us understand how you use our platform (with your consent).

| Cookie Name | Purpose | Duration | Type |
|-------------|---------|----------|------|
| `cc_analytics` | Platform usage analytics | 1 year | Analytics |
| `cc_session` | Session tracking | 30 minutes | Analytics |
| `cc_events` | Event tracking | 7 days | Analytics |

### Performance Cookies
These cookies help us monitor and improve platform performance.

| Cookie Name | Purpose | Duration | Type |
|-------------|---------|----------|------|
| `performance-monitoring` | Error tracking | 24 hours | Performance |
| `load-time-tracking` | Performance metrics | 1 hour | Performance |

## Managing Your Cookie Preferences

### Browser Settings
You can control cookies through your browser settings:

- **Chrome**: Settings > Privacy and Security > Cookies and other site data
- **Firefox**: Settings > Privacy & Security > Cookies and Site Data
- **Safari**: Preferences > Privacy > Manage Website Data
- **Edge**: Settings > Cookies and Site Permissions

### Platform Settings
You can manage non-essential cookies in your ClickChutney account:

1. Go to **Settings** in your dashboard
2. Navigate to **Privacy Preferences**
3. Toggle analytics and performance cookies
4. Save your preferences

### Opt-Out Options
- **Analytics Cookies**: Disable in account settings or browser
- **Third-Party Cookies**: Use browser settings or third-party opt-out tools
- **All Cookies**: Use browser's private/incognito mode

## Impact of Disabling Cookies

### Essential Cookies
Disabling essential cookies will prevent you from:
- Logging into your account
- Accessing dashboard features
- Maintaining session security

### Non-Essential Cookies
Disabling non-essential cookies may result in:
- Loss of personalized settings
- Reduced platform performance insights
- Need to reconfigure preferences frequently

## Mobile Apps and Local Storage

Our platform may use local storage and similar technologies on mobile devices:

- **Local Storage**: For offline functionality and caching
- **Session Storage**: For temporary data during your session
- **IndexedDB**: For client-side database storage

## Cookie Consent

### Consent Management
- We obtain consent for non-essential cookies before setting them
- You can withdraw consent at any time through your account settings
- Consent is recorded and can be reviewed in your privacy settings

### Legal Basis
Our legal basis for using cookies:
- **Essential Cookies**: Legitimate interest for platform functionality
- **Analytics Cookies**: Consent for usage analysis
- **Marketing Cookies**: Consent for personalized experiences

## Data Transfers

Some cookies may involve data transfers to countries outside your region:
- We ensure adequate protection through appropriate safeguards
- Data is transferred only to providers with strong privacy commitments
- You can object to such transfers through your account settings

## Technical Information

### Cookie Security
- Cookies are transmitted over secure HTTPS connections
- Sensitive cookies are marked as Secure and HttpOnly
- We use SameSite attributes to prevent cross-site request forgery

### Cookie Storage
- Cookies are stored locally on your device
- We do not access cookies set by other websites
- Our cookies are domain-specific to our platform
